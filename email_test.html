3. Gmail Setup Checklist

  • Enable 2-Factor Authentication on your Google account
  • Generate an App Password (not your regular Gmail password)
  • Update school_settings with correct email and encrypted App Password
  • Ensure encryption keys are properly configured in config.php
  • Test with a valid email address (e.g., user@gmail.com)

4. Common Issues & Solutions

Username/Password not accepted
Use App Password instead of regular Gmail password
Connection timeout
Check firewall settings and internet connection
Decryption failed
Update encryption keys in config.php and re-encrypt passwords
No school settings found
Add email configuration to school_settings table
Invalid email format
Ensure email address includes @ symbol and valid domain
